    <h2 class="post-title"><a href="http://evie-s.com/news/2010/04/26/diy-lace-earrings/" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to DIY Lace Earrings">DIY Lace Earrings</a></h2>

    <div class="post-meta">
	    <span class="post-author">
		    by		    <a href="http://evie-s.com/news/author/evies./" title="Posts by evie s." rel="author">evie s.</a>	    </span><!-- .post-author -->

	    <span class="post-date">
		on		April 26, 2010,
	    </span><!-- .post-author -->

	    <span class="post-comments">
		<a href="http://evie-s.com/news/2010/04/26/diy-lace-earrings/#comments">16 comments</a>
	    </span><!-- .post-author -->
    </div><!-- END .post-meta -->

    	
    
    <div class="post-content"><p>Hi all, I hope everyone had a great weekend. I&#8217;m recovering from a sleepless night&#8230;my son&#8217;s first fever EVER kept us all up most of the night last night. So a nap may be in order later today! For now, I have a quick and easy project to share with you!</p>
<p>These are super cute and so easy to make. You need: 2 plain medium sized buttons, lace, earring studs (found at any craft store), and a glue gun.</p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1432" title="earrings1" src="http://evie-s.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2010/04/earrings1.jpg" alt="" width="571" height="428" /></p>
<p>Start by cutting a piece of lace larger than your button. Then start folding the fabric around the button, sort of wrapping it around like you are wrapping a present and gluing as you go.</p>
