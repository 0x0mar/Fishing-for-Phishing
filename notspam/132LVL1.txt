<p><a href="http://evie-s.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2007/11/bird.jpg" title="bird.jpg" rel="image_group"></a>The <a target="_blank" href="http://www.redenvelope.com/re/gifts/product_display/product_information.jsp?nc=7993&#038;refPg=endeca&#038;oid=25321459&#038;nc2=1">Graphite Writing Sculptures</a> by San Francisco artist Agelio Batle are beautiful and practical (my favorite combination). These sculptural beauties are made of real graphite that write like pencils and they can double as an eye-pleasing paperweight. They are available in three different versions: morel mushroom, olive branch and a leaf.</p>
<p><img src="http://evie-s.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2007/11/bird.jpg" alt="bird.jpg" />The second rusticly-wonderful pick is this <a target="_blank" href="http://www.potterybarn.com/products/p9787/index.cfm?pkey=ggftggfher">Bird Jewelry Holder</a> from <a target="_blank" href="http://www.potterybarn.com">Pottery Barn</a>. Typically I don&#8217;t shop at PB but as I was browsing through their catalog, this really caught my eye. I love the idea of putting the day&#8217;s jewelry to rest on this cast aluminum creation. And what a bargain at only $19.</p>
<p>There is alot more out there in the way of rustic accents that you can add to your interior spaces. Keep an open mind and look for clean and simple forms made of uncomplicated materials and you can do no wrong. Our Earth is the ultimate creator of beauty!</p>
