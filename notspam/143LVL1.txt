		<div id="post-310" class="post-310 post type-post status-publish format-standard hentry category-things-of-beauty tag-australian-artist tag-illustrations tag-kareena-zerefos">
    <h2 class="post-title"><a href="http://evie-s.com/news/2008/10/24/illustrations-by-kareena-zerefos/" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to Illustrations by Kareena Zerefos">Illustrations by Kareena Zerefos</a></h2>

    <div class="post-meta">
	    <span class="post-author">
		    by		    <a href="http://evie-s.com/news/author/evies./" title="Posts by evie s." rel="author">evie s.</a>	    </span><!-- .post-author -->

	    <span class="post-date">
		on		October 24, 2008,
	    </span><!-- .post-author -->

	    <span class="post-comments">
		<a href="http://evie-s.com/news/2008/10/24/illustrations-by-kareena-zerefos/#comments">0 comments</a>
	    </span><!-- .post-author -->
    </div><!-- END .post-meta -->

    	
    
    <div class="post-content"><p>These dreamy <a href="http://www.kareenazerefos.com/illustrations.html" target="_blank">illustrations</a> by Australian artist, <a href="http://www.kareenazerefos.com" target="_blank">Kareena Zerefos</a> are so lovely. Kareena creates a bittersweet, slightly unsettling feeling in her work by exploring themes of isolation and escapism. She works using a variety of media, including pencil, gouache and ink, and often combines traditional drawing with digital graphics. You can <a href="http://www.kareenazerefos.com/index.php" target="_blank">purchase some of her work</a> as limited edition giclee prints.</p>
