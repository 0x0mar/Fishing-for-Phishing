    <h2 class="post-title"><a href="http://evie-s.com/news/2009/09/30/20-questions/" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to 20 Questions">20 Questions</a></h2>

    <div class="post-meta">

    <div class="post-content"><p>Goodness it&#8217;s already Wednesday and I haven&#8217;t posted this week. It&#8217;s been really busy, lots going on. But, I thought it would be fun to do a little 20 Questions post. Here goes!</p>
<p><strong>1. Show us the inside of something cute.</strong> I love this little trinket that&#8217;s in my sons room of a jungle scene. Cute decoration only? Nope! Open it up and it reveals pieces for a game of Tiddlywinks.</p>
