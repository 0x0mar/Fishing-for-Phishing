<p>This is a <b>list of notable blogs</b>. A <a href="/wiki/Blog" title="Blog">blog</a> (contraction of weblog) is a web site with frequent, periodic posts creating an ongoing narrative. They are maintained by both groups and individuals, the latter being the most common. Blogs can focus on a wide variety of topics, ranging from the political to personal experiences. Specific blogs include:</p>
