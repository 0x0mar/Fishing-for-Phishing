    <h2 class="post-title"><a href="http://evie-s.com/news/2010/01/29/inspiration-art-with-paper/" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to Inspiration: Art with Paper">Inspiration: Art with Paper</a></h2>

    <div class="post-meta">
	    <span class="post-author">
		    by		    <a href="http://evie-s.com/news/author/evies./" title="Posts by evie s." rel="author">evie s.</a>	    </span><!-- .post-author -->

    <h2 class="post-title"><a href="http://evie-s.com/news/2010/01/20/artist-interview-danni-of-oh-hello-friend/" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to Artist Interview: Danni of Oh, Hello Friend">Artist Interview: Danni of Oh, Hello Friend</a></h2>

    <div class="post-meta">
	    <span class="post-author">
		    by		    <a href="http://evie-s.com/news/author/evies./" title="Posts by evie s." rel="author">evie s.</a>	    </span><!-- .post-author -->

	    <span class="post-date">
		on		January 20, 2010,
	    </span><!-- .post-author -->

	    <span class="post-comments">
		<a href="http://evie-s.com/news/2010/01/20/artist-interview-danni-of-oh-hello-friend/#comments">2 comments</a>
	    </span><!-- .post-author -->
    </div><!-- END .post-meta -->

    	
    
    <div class="post-content"><p>Our first artist interview of the new year is with the always inspiring Danni, of <a href="http://ohhellofriend.blogspot.com/" target="_blank">Oh, Hello Friend</a>. Hers is one of my favorite blog reads and she is so talented in the areas of art, design, photography and packaging with a wonderful eye for detail. Make sure you visit her <a href="http://www.etsy.com/shop/ohhellofriend" target="_blank">etsy shop</a> too!</p>
<p><a href="http://evie-s.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/danni1.jpg" rel="image_group"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1228" title="danni" src="http://evie-s.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/danni1.jpg" alt="" width="571" height="155" /></a></p>
<p><strong>1. Tell us about yourself.</strong><br />
My name is Danni and I love to design and blog! I am recently engaged and have now entered into the world of <a href="http://ohhellofriend.blogspot.com/2009/11/proposal-story.html" target="_blank">WEDDING PLANNING</a>! yikes! I really love traveling, designing, being creative, and would love to work at an orphanage someday.</p>
<p><a href="http://evie-s.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/danni11.jpg" rel="image_group"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1229" title="danni1" src="http://evie-s.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/danni11.jpg" alt="" width="571" height="242" /></a></p>
<h5><a href="http://ohhellofriend.blogspot.com/2009/11/proposal-story.html" target="_blank">Proposal Story</a> and <a href="http://ohhellofriend.blogspot.com/2009/10/bridesmaid-projects.html" target="_blank">Bridesmaids Projects</a></h5>
<p><strong>2. What do you do to stay inspired and motivated to create?</strong><br />
When I need inspiration, I sometimes take a couple hours and go read magazines at Borders. Even though the magazine industry seems to be fading with ever-growing blogs, there is still something really nice about sitting and looking through something you can hold and flip through. I will sometimes bring a notebook and sketch down ideas or inspiration from the magazines. It&#8217;s of course inspiring to check out all my favorite blogs and look for new projects or ideas.</p>
<p><a href="http://evie-s.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/danni2.jpg" rel="image_group"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1230" title="danni2" src="http://evie-s.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/danni2.jpg" alt="" width="571" height="231" /></a></p>
<h5><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/ohhellofriend/3837248696/" target="_blank">Favorite</a> <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/ohhellofriend/3837248604/" target="_blank">Things</a></h5>
<p><strong>3. What are some other passions you have besides art and design?</strong><br />
Well, like I said in #1, I really do love to travel! I&#8217;ve been to a few different countries and it is really the best to get out and explore the world beyond what I am just used to from my day to day. I  also really love going to thrift stores and flea markets &#8211; it&#8217;s fun to be able to discover a great find or &#8216;treasure&#8217; and collect things. (Although my fiancee would say I collect too many things and don&#8217;t need anything else, hehe.)</p>
<p><a href="http://evie-s.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/danni3.jpg" rel="image_group"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1231" title="danni3" src="http://evie-s.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/danni3.jpg" alt="" width="571" height="187" /></a></p>
<h5><a href="http://www.etsy.com/view_listing.php?listing_id=38494645" target="_blank">lovely vintage hair pin set</a> and <a href="http://www.etsy.com/view_listing.php?listing_id=38010662" target="_blank">keep calm and carry on pocket mirror</a></h5>
<p><strong>4. Give us a glimpse into a normal day in the life of Danni. </strong><br />
A normal day varies from day to day but always consists of blogging. Other things I&#8217;ll work on is make new jewelry for my etsy shop, package whatever etsy orders I have, run errands, and work on whatever needs to be done for the day. When I am preparing for a craft show though: that looks a lot different. My apartment becomes a mess and looks like a hurricane hit it. There are supplies everywhere as I frantically am rushing to get enough stuff made for the show. hehe, I am definitely not by any means a &#8216;neat&#8217; worker.</p>
<p><a href="http://evie-s.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/danni4.jpg" rel="image_group"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1232" title="danni4" src="http://evie-s.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/danni4.jpg" alt="" width="571" height="203" /></a></p>
<h5><a href="http://ohhellofriend.blogspot.com/search/label/packaging" target="_blank">Lovely Packaging</a></h5>
<p><strong>5. I love your <a href="http://ohhellofriend101in1001.blogspot.com/" target="_blank">101 in 1001 goals list</a>. Are there any that you are working on right now?</strong><br />
Thank you! I was really inspired to make a list after seeing my friend make one. Life is a little busy right now, I&#8217;d say the only thing I am really working on at this point is planning the wedding. I really want to do so much more in the art and business section (like update my portfolio and start a new shop) but I just don&#8217;t have the time, maybe in 2011!</p>
<p><a href="http://evie-s.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/danni5.jpg" rel="image_group"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1233" title="danni5" src="http://evie-s.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/danni5.jpg" alt="" width="571" height="209" /></a></p>
<h5>Unique <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/ohhellofriend/4169890481/" target="_blank">craft</a> <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/ohhellofriend/4169890317/" target="_blank">show</a>, Los Angeles</h5>
<p><strong>6. Lastly, tell us something random or quirky about yourself!</strong><br />
Random.. hm. I don&#8217;t like cheese! and quirky..sometimes when it rains (which it doesn&#8217;t often rain in orange county) I&#8217;ll just go sit out in my car with a good book and the back hatch open so I can look out into it and read in the rain. I love the rain.</p>
<p><a href="http://evie-s.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/danni6.jpg" rel="image_group"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1234" title="danni6" src="http://evie-s.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/danni6.jpg" alt="" width="571" height="231" /></a></p>

    <div class="post-content"><p>I decided to share a cute little sewing project I finished the other day. On a whim I decided I wanted a new cardigan with a spring feel, you know, because of the sub-zero temperatures lately. Ha! Anyway, I&#8217;m dreaming of Spring and what other color to incorporate than yellow, right? So I took a sweater that I don&#8217;t really wear anymore and decided to make it into a cardigan.</p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1202" title="sweater1" src="http://evie-s.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/sweater1.jpg" alt="sweater1" width="571" height="428" /></p>
<p>I sliced up the front to create an opening. Then I cut a piece of yellow fabric, folded it in half and sewed the raw edge (because I don&#8217;t like fraying) and did a gathering stitch by hand to create a ruffle. My sewing machine was on the fritz, otherwise I just would have done it that way. I pinned my ruffle in place and used vintage bias tape to finish both edges and sewed everything down. A vintage button finishes the piece.</p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1203" title="sweater2" src="http://evie-s.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/sweater2.jpg" alt="sweater2" width="571" height="428" /></p>
<p>I decided to just do one button, so it will be more versatile for my burgeoning belly (17 weeks now, yay!). I love the yellow hues, it makes me so happy! And yes, that yellow is as electric as it looks in the photos. *Smile*</p>
<p>Anyone else ready for Spring?</p>

    <div class="post-content"><p>Happy 2010 everyone! I trust you had a nice break and are raring to go for another year, and&#8230;another decade!</p>
<p>I&#8217;ve been experimenting with some projects made of felt that I thought I&#8217;d quickly share. For some reason I&#8217;ve been on a kick of making things for myself which for some reason, I rarely do. Maybe it&#8217;s because I truly enjoy creating more when it&#8217;s for someone else. But, for now I&#8217;ve been enjoying the fruits of my own labor!</p>
<p>Part of my husband and I&#8217;s Christmas gifts to each other were brand <a href="http://www.google.com/url?sa=t&amp;source=web&amp;ct=res&amp;cd=2&amp;ved=0CBUQFjAB&amp;url=http%3A%2F%2Fphones.verizonwireless.com%2Fmotorola%2Fdroid%2F&amp;rct=j&amp;q=droid+phone&amp;ei=Z61ES6f3PJTMNYvIzPEB&amp;usg=AFQjCNF0kSJranmP-HMzofSEsy65kqce8A" mce_href="http://www.google.com/url?sa=t&amp;source=web&amp;ct=res&amp;cd=2&amp;ved=0CBUQFjAB&amp;url=http%3A%2F%2Fphones.verizonwireless.com%2Fmotorola%2Fdroid%2F&amp;rct=j&amp;q=droid+phone&amp;ei=Z61ES6f3PJTMNYvIzPEB&amp;usg=AFQjCNF0kSJranmP-HMzofSEsy65kqce8A" target="_blank">new phones</a>. Mine is so stinking new on the market, they don&#8217;t have many options in the way of cases or protection. So I made my own! I think it turned out very cute, and now my phone is nice and cozy.</p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1184" title="felt1" src="http://evie-s.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/felt1.jpg" mce_src="http://evie-s.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/felt1.jpg" alt="felt1" width="571" height="428"></p>
<p>I also experimented with a little felt brooch. It&#8217;s not marvelous, but it&#8217;s cute enough. Experimentation leads to innovation, right?</p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1185" title="felt2" src="http://evie-s.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/felt2.jpg" mce_src="http://evie-s.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/felt2.jpg" alt="felt2" width="571" height="428"></p>
