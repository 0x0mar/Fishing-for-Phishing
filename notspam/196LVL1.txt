    <h2 class="post-title"><a href="http://evie-s.com/news/2013/07/16/ftm-illustrations/" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to FTM Illustrations">FTM Illustrations</a></h2>

    <div class="post-meta">
	    <span class="post-author">
		    by		    <a href="http://evie-s.com/news/author/evies./" title="Posts by evie s." rel="author">evie s.</a>	    </span><!-- .post-author -->

	    <span class="post-date">
		on		July 16, 2013,
	    </span><!-- .post-author -->

	    <span class="post-comments">
		<a href="http://evie-s.com/news/2013/07/16/ftm-illustrations/#comments">2 comments</a>

    <div class="post-content"><p>This month <a href="http://www.forthemakers.com">For the Makers</a> unveiled a new website and with it some nifty people illustrations of the team. Katie, Janet, Rachel, and myself are presented in pretty watercolors and ink for the very first time. This illustration project was a good challenge &#8211; not too fashiony and harsh, but still chic enough to fit in with the rest of the FTM style.</p>
<p><a href="http://evie-s.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/FTMteam2.jpg" rel="image_group"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3543" alt="FTMteam" src="http://evie-s.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/FTMteam2.jpg" width="601" height="684" /></a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Of course, I had to include the freckles on my nose and cheeks that come out to play every summer. :)</p>
