    <h2 class="post-title"><a href="http://evie-s.com/news/2007/12/28/a-dramatic-mobile-centerpiece/" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to A Dramatic Mobile Centerpiece">A Dramatic Mobile Centerpiece</a></h2>

    <div class="post-meta">
	    <span class="post-author">
		    by		    <a href="http://evie-s.com/news/author/evies./" title="Posts by evie s." rel="author">evie s.</a>	    </span><!-- .post-author -->

    <div class="post-content"><p><img src="http://evie-s.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2007/12/mobile.jpg" alt="A Dramatic Mobile Centerpiece" align="right" />I recently stumbled across <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/potterfamily/2110818172/" target="_blank">this photo</a> of a hand-crafted mobile made from some branches, silver wire and paper that&#8217;s been cut into star shapes. This idea is so fresh and unique and would add a lot of drama to a holiday table. A New Years Eve party would be a perfect setting for it. There are also many different ways to add flair: Spray paint the branches gold or silver, use colored or patterned paper, or use different shapes for the hanging ornaments.</p>
<p>See the full-size photo <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/potterfamily/2110818172/" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>
