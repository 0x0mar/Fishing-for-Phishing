<p>In Wikipedia and other <a href="/wiki/MediaWiki" title="MediaWiki">MediaWiki</a> projects, <b><a href="/wiki/Special:Random" title="Special:Random">Special:Random</a></b> can be used to access a random article in the main <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Namespace" title="Wikipedia:Namespace">namespace</a>; this feature is useful as a general starting point for browsing Wikipedia or if you just want to learn a random piece of information.</p>
<p>Depending on your browser, it's also possible to load a random page using a <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Keyboard_shortcuts" title="Wikipedia:Keyboard shortcuts">keyboard shortcut</a> (in Firefox and Chrome <kbd class="keyboard-key nowrap" style="border: 1px solid #aaa; -moz-border-radius: 0.2em; -webkit-border-radius: 0.2em; border-radius: 0.2em; -moz-box-shadow: 0.1em 0.2em 0.2em #ddd; -webkit-box-shadow: 0.1em 0.2em 0.2em #ddd; box-shadow: 0.1em 0.2em 0.2em #ddd; background-color: #f9f9f9; background-image: -moz-linear-gradient(top, #eee, #f9f9f9, #eee); background-image: -o-linear-gradient(top, #eee, #f9f9f9, #eee); background-image: -webkit-linear-gradient(top, #eee, #f9f9f9, #eee); background-image: linear-gradient(to bottom, #eee, #f9f9f9, #eee); padding: 0.1em 0.3em; font-family: inherit; font-size: 0.85em;">Alt-Shift</kbd>+<kbd class="keyboard-key nowrap" style="border: 1px solid #aaa; -moz-border-radius: 0.2em; -webkit-border-radius: 0.2em; border-radius: 0.2em; -moz-box-shadow: 0.1em 0.2em 0.2em #ddd; -webkit-box-shadow: 0.1em 0.2em 0.2em #ddd; box-shadow: 0.1em 0.2em 0.2em #ddd; background-color: #f9f9f9; background-image: -moz-linear-gradient(top, #eee, #f9f9f9, #eee); background-image: -o-linear-gradient(top, #eee, #f9f9f9, #eee); background-image: -webkit-linear-gradient(top, #eee, #f9f9f9, #eee); background-image: linear-gradient(to bottom, #eee, #f9f9f9, #eee); padding: 0.1em 0.3em; font-family: inherit; font-size: 0.85em;">X</kbd>).</p>
<p>To create a link to a random page in a different <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Namespace" title="Wikipedia:Namespace">namespace</a>, use <code>[[Special:Random/<i>Namespace</i>]]</code>.</p>
<p>For example, <b><a href="/wiki/Special:Random/Talk" title="Special:Random/Talk">Special:Random/Talk</a></b> will take you to a random article's talk page.</p>
